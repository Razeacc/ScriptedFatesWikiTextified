== Overview ==
Archkin are beings made of two parts: '''Soul''' and '''Faith''' tied together with '''Frequencies'''.

=== Soul Energy ===
The [[PSYCHE (Soul)|SOUL]] comes in two halves: [[Memory]] and [[Sentience]]. They use [[PSYCHE (Soul)|Soul Energy]] as the '''building blocks''' for their capabilities in the form of "''Archose''", a <u>dust-like substance</u> that makes up their shapeshifting form. This is a '''heightened extension''' from how [[memories]] act as the building blocks for [[PSYCHE (Soul)|soul]] energy. 

This means they '''gain more soul energy''' the <u>more [[memories]]</u> and <u>experiences</u> they obtain. 

But in contrast, how does an Archkin ''heighten their [[Sentience]]'' to match the increased [[memories]]? Archkin eventually becomes cognitively unbalanced when overloaded with <u>[[memories]]</u> while lacking the [[sentience]] stability to interpret them. There is a way to heighten [[sentience]].

=== Faith ===
[[Faith]] is a '''resource''' innate in <u>anything containing a soul</u>. It's just that Archkin are more capable of tapping into it. By <u>challenging their confidence</u> and '''succeeding''', they gain [[Faith]] in themselves. This '''heightens their [[sentience]]''', increasing their overall prowess and capabilities. By completing a god-like feat, this is also what elevates an Archkin's [[Archkin#Archose|archose]], ascending it '''even further''', and achieving [[Archgods|Archgod]]<nowiki/>hood.

Although, a word of caution is in store. As their [[sentience]] heightens, their emotionality deepens as well— making them more susceptible to overwhelming states such as '''[[Archkin#The Pandemonium State|Pandemonium]]'''.

=== Frequencies ===
All Archkin resonate at '''unique frequencies''', similar to how [[PSYCHE (Soul)|SOULs]] and their natural/resonant frequencies. If <u>humans</u> have '''''DNA''''', <u>Archkin</u> have '''''frequencies'''''. These frequencies form an invisible protective field around them, '''shielding them from physical harm'''. However, when that frequency is disturbed by external stimuli, it <u>weakens</u> their protection, but only ''slightly''.<br />Their <u>true weaknesses</u> resides in the {{Nul Sym}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|'''Phantasmal''']] '''Frequency''' of '''2413Hz''' and {{4th Sym}} [[You|'''Your''']] '''3142Hz:''' the <u>separatist frequencies</u>.

When afflicted by any of those frequencies, it forcibly <u>tears apart their body's archose</u> and <u>weakens their frequency shell</u>. The feeling is akin to being shocked by electricity while burning alive.<br />During this state, that is the '''only time''' an archkin may suffer '''finality'''. '''Frequencies''' act as the '''bond''' uniting an Archkin's [[PSYCHE (Soul)|Soul]] and [[Faith]] together. But when faced with ''separation'', it becomes their '''greatest weakness'''.

== Abilities ==

=== Manipulation of [[The 4 Aspects of Reality]] {{AspectIcon|1}}{{AspectIcon|2}}{{AspectIcon|3}}{{AspectIcon|4}} ===
By using their body's [[Archkin#Archose|archose]] physiology, they can '''manipulate reality''' through various manners. <u>Heating up ground</u>, <u>splitting air</u>, <u>capturing sentience</u>, and even <u>rewriting memories</u>. They are essentially made of "magic", some may describe it. There is virtually nothing they can't do <small>(a misconception that was the leading-cause for [[The Revolutions and Exodi Era|The Archgod Revolution]]).</small>

=== Shapeshifting{{AspectIcon|1}} ===
All Archkin can innately manipulate their forms using the [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Space|{{AspectIcon|1}}Space]] aspect. By shaping their body's [[Archkin#Archose|archose]] into various forms, they can take on a variety of '''shapes''', '''colors''', and '''sizes'''. Some do it to <u>express themselves</u>, others do it to hide.

This means <u>clothing</u> worn by Archkin are merely '''extensions''' of their body, woven from [[Archkin#Archose|archose]].

{{#SQuote:|text=Yes... that means all archkin are technically naked...<br />but don't call it that, they have nothing to hide anyways, it's FINE!|speaker=[[RAZEACC|Rayzie]]|receiver=[[You]]|attribution=|source=[[Archkin|while you read this page]]|id=W7RI-1RM0-612Z|template=}}

=== Psyche Planes ===
With enough [[Faith]], an Archkin can access and manipulate the '''pocket dimension''' within their own soul. Time does not pass normally in this plane, and it acts as a representative culmination of the archkin's personal experiences, [[Memories|memory]], and self. That is the '''Psyche Plane'''.<br>Normally, the plane is a '''barren white void''' with an invisible floor. But as one spends more time in it, the space '''alters''' the user's inner being.

This plane is typically used for deep thought and meditation, being a space free from the flow of time itself. However, as part of Archkin culture, this space is also often used for '''[[Psyche Duel|Psyche Duels]]'''.

When making physical contact with an Archkin, they may drag your [[PSYCHE (Soul)|soul]] into their Psyche Plane. Your [[PSYCHE (Soul)|soul]] would take on the form of your original body as you duel against the archkin.<br />
<u>Physical harm within a plane does '''not''' transfer into reality</u>, which is what made it perfect for '''[[Psyche Duel|Psyche Duels]]''' in Archkin Culture. Instead, once defeated, the loser would return to reality <u>completely exhausted</u> and with <u>blurry [[Memories|memories]]</u> for some time. This lack of long-lasting consequences is what made the culture thrive and prosper.
<br /><small>That is, unless you're dealing with [[Khueen|The Strongest Archgod]]. There is a reason few dared to challenge her, even fewer living to share what her Psyche Plane is like, and barely anyone knowing the ''truth'' of 'her plane'.</small>

== Biology and Physiology ==
As Archkin only get stronger from ''[[memories]]'' and [[Faith|''faith'']], that means they <u>don't need traditional sleep</u> and <u>food consumption</u> akin to mortals. They can still taste, but '''nothing conventional stops an Archkin from living indefinitely'''. However, they do still get fatigued when manipulating reality.

When in combat against people who are <u>unaware of an Archkin's weakness</u>, and are <u>trying to stay hidden</u> themselves, Archkin would often '''fake damage''' to avoid suspicion.<br />They'd shapeshift their archose to form <u>fake bruises</u>, spit out archose-molded <u>blood</u>, maybe even <u>overreact the pain</u>. Sometimes, if they're sloppy, you may even notice an Archkin <u>reacting to an attack a '''split second''' before actually feeling it</u>.<br />Once the coast is clear, they would just shapeshift back to their normal forms, and escape.

As for the traits of an Archkin's body, there are 4 distinct ones.

=== Frequency Shell ===
<blockquote>The most important part of an archkin's body. Due to their immense power, their true appearance is ''capable of shattering the fabric of space and time'' itself.<br /><small>Please refer to "[[The Fall of Marlika]]" for confirmation.</small>

As such, their frequency shell protects both '''themselves''' from <u>harm</u>, and '''us''' from <u>instant annihilation</u>. The frequency shell wraps around an entire archkin's body, encapsulating each and every [[Archkin#Archose|archose]] particle, similar to a thin bubble. It is invisible under normal circumstances.</blockquote>
=== Frequency Weakness ===
<blockquote>An Archkin's '''main weakness''' comes in the form of <u>frequencies</u>. When exposed to external stimuli that meddle with the delicate vibrations of their [[Archkin#Biology and Physiology#Frequency Shell|frequency shells]], it produces varying levels of '''discomfort'''.
These stimuli can range from <u>loud noises</u> at <u>high/low pitches</u>, <u>flames</u>, <u>electricity</u>, <u>music,</u> or [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|phantasmal]] phenomena. But when under specifically '''2413Hz''' or '''3142Hz''', as felt in the [[Fracturing Sea]], the discomfort heightens to an '''electrifying'''/'''burning''' sensation that directly '''destabilizes their shell''' and seperates the bonds of their [[Archkin#Archose|archose]] <small>(hence why they're called the "Separatist" Frequencies)</small>, even to the point of risking the archkin's finality.</blockquote>

=== Intended Sterility ===
<blockquote>As Archkin were '''never invented for times of peace''', they were never designed with self-replication in-mind.

Archkin attempting to defy this decree by creating more Archkin is the <u>most severe act of defiance</u> against [[The Court of The 4th]].<br /><small>Please refer to "The Trial and Banishment of Archgod [[Khueen]] and Archgod [[Ouya]], and Archgod [[Kalayan]]"</small></blockquote>

=== Finality ===
<blockquote>The end of an Archkin is akin to a <u>star's supernova</u>.
<br />As an Archkin sustains damage, their [[Archkin#Biology and Physiology#Frequency Shell|frequency shell]] begins to crack, forming bright '''glitchy lights''' around the wound. These glitches are the <u>fabrics of space and time</u> physically '''bending''' due to the Archkin's true form leaking out.
Upon reaching finality, an archkin's [[Archkin#Biology and Physiology#Frequency Shell|shell]] implodes on itself in a final attempt to subdue as much of their innate power as possible. After the implosion, the remaining archose within the Archkin bursts out in an explosion of pure [[PSYCHE (Soul)|soul]] energy, burning and shredding anything nearby.
<br />Any disconnected [[Archkin#Archose|archose]] from that archkin would remain in the world, still containing its [[Memories|memories]], but without the ability for the archkin to control it.</blockquote>

== Archose ==
[[Category:Bestiary]]
<blockquote><small>''Etymology: (Archaic + Glucose = '''Archose''')''</small></blockquote>A dust-like particle that comprises all archkin's bodies. If [[PSYCHE (Soul)]] is known as "The Zenith Energy" to some, Archose is known as "The God Particle". Not only is '''ascended [[PSYCHE (Soul)|soul]] energy''', it also acts as the building blocks of matter itself.

Atoms, molecules, '''everything''' was once an archkin's archose. It merely adapts and changes over time as a living ''reprogrammable'' form of matter. This substance is how Archkin can manipulate [[The 4 Aspects of Reality]].<br />
Another trait of Archose is that it functions as '''all 5 senses'''. Archkin can <u>hear</u>, <u>smell</u>, <u>touch</u>, and <u>taste</u> from their Archose, with only archose dedicated to forming '''eyes''' being able to <u>see</u> out of.

=== Origins of Mana ===
In many universes, magical phenomena is a common feature. In many of those worlds, that arcane features can be attributed to a random Archkin who travelled that world long ago and left their archose behind (<small>a common pass-time of archkin back in [[The Archaic Aeons Era]])</small>.

When that archose mutates to the world it was left in, traces of that archkin's abilities manifest and festers into a different, but similar, phenomena. That is what many of these worlds call "'''Mana'''". People with <u>innate magical abilities</u> have '''trace doses of ancient archose''' imbued in their very [[PSYCHE (Soul)|soul]]. "Magical Phenomena" is the user utilizing their trace archose to manipulate the world.

== The Pandemonium State ==
<blockquote>As Archkin are innately connected to the very concept of [[PSYCHE (Soul)|souls]] and the [[memories]] within them, those building blocks can often be '''stimulated''' to a dangerous degree. 
When intense emotions flare up within an Archkin, the [[Memories|memory]] tied to those emotions can cause an <u>overwhelming increase in '''power'''</u> and <u>loss of '''control'''</u>. That is '''Pandemonium'''.<small>Please refer to "[[The Outpost 7 Catastrophe]]" and Archkin [[Archie]]'s hindered attempt at ascending to [[Archgod]]<nowiki/>hood.</small></blockquote>

==== How to deal with a Pandemonic Archkin? ====
<blockquote>Due to the average person's capabilities and resources, the <u>most common</u> course of action would be to '''escape''' or '''hide''' from the Archkin's attention as much as possible, especially when the flared-up emotion is anger. 
However, if a <u>capable archkin</u> is present, or with the right equipment, it may be best to '''face''' the pandemonic archkin in a '''[[Psyche Duel]]''' to tire them out near-instantly. But do be prepared, as you would be facing the eye of the storm, a pandemonic archkin is even more powerful during a '''[[Psyche Duel]]'''.</blockquote>
