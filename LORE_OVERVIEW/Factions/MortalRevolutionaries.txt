Chatty's Memory Summary:

The Mortal Revolutionaries are a secretive inter-firmata organization publicly known under various regional names. Their true name, the Arch of Sentients (AoS), is intentionally concealed from most civilizations and even many lower-ranking members within the organization itself.

Originally formed by mortals seeking protection from reality-altering entities known as Archkin, the AoS operates as both a containment organization and a philosophical movement. While the majority of its members are mortal, the organization is known to contain rare Archkin defectors who believe their own species poses an existential threat to civilization.

The AoS believes that unrestricted Archkin influence inevitably destabilizes reality, civilization, memory, and history itself. As a result, their primary mission is to locate, contain, suppress, monitor, or eliminate dangerous Archkin activity across The Worlds Aplenty.

The organization draws inspiration from:
- Esoteric bureaucracies
- Paramilitary containment agencies
- Religious anti-divinity movements
- Psychological experimentation programs
- Multiversal disaster management systems

One of the AoS's most disturbing methods involves the use of "emptied timelines" for containment. Captured Archkin may be imprisoned within isolated or abandoned timelines stripped of civilization, history, or meaningful stimuli in order to reduce the risk of reality destabilization.

Despite their stated mission of protecting civilization, the AoS is morally controversial. Critics accuse the organization of:
- Timeline manipulation
- Historical censorship
- Psychological conditioning
- Artificial memory alteration
- Civilizational experimentation
- Cross-world interference
- Extreme paranoia regarding fictional contamination

A recurring question surrounding the organization is whether they genuinely protect reality... or whether they fear losing control over it.
