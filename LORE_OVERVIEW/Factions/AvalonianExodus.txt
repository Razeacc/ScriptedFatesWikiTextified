Chatty's Memory Summary:

The Avalonian Exodus refers to a massive historical migration event that occurred following the collapse, abandonment, or destabilization of Avalon. Although records surrounding the event are fragmented and often contradictory, the Exodus is considered one of the defining turning points in the history of several civilizations across The Worlds Aplenty.

Many surviving accounts describe the event less as a normal evacuation and more as a cultural rupture. Entire populations vanished, scattered across distant territories, timelines, or firmatas while carrying fragmented traditions, technologies, myths, and psychological scars with them.

Because of this, traces of Avalonian influence can appear in wildly disconnected regions despite those regions possessing no obvious historical contact.

The Exodus is heavily associated with:
- Lost technological knowledge
- Memory corruption
- Ruin megastructures
- Cultural fragmentation
- Artificial ecosystems
- Interdimensional migration
- Mythologized survivors
- Contradictory historical records

Some historians believe Avalon collapsed due to external invasion or internal war. Others argue the civilization attempted to escape something far worse. More extreme theories suggest the Exodus itself may have been intentional, designed to scatter knowledge before a larger reality-scale catastrophe occurred.

The true nature of Avalon remains unclear.

In many modern discussions, the Avalonian Exodus is viewed simultaneously as:
- A historical disaster
- A mythological origin point
- A conspiracy subject
- A religious symbol
- A warning about uncontrolled advancement

Certain factions within the Scripted Fates universe continue searching for evidence regarding what truly happened during the Exodus, believing the answer may fundamentally reshape humanity's understanding of reality itself.
