== Overview ==
'''Sentience''' is a resource <u>embodied by anything and everything</u> that can '''think''' and '''choose''' for oneself. It is what contains and utilizes ⁠[[Memories]] in the formation of ⁠[[PSYCHE (Soul)|PSYCHE Energy]] ('''Soul''').

'''Quantity''' determines the <u>amount of memories</u> it can manage.<br />'''Quality''' dictates <u>how well</u> it can process and perceive [[memories]], playing closely to a Memory's [[Memories#Memory Amplitude|'''Amplitude''']].
Alongside its capability of processing memories, higher <u>categories of Sentience</u> slowly opens up one's '''[[Firmata's Rolling System#Hidden Stats|Lucidity]]''', allowing glimpses at the '''[[Beyonds]]'''. <blockquote><small>Sentience is governed by the [[NulGraven]] half of {{Phantasy}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|Phantasy]].</small></blockquote>

== Extracting Sentience ==
Sentience can be extracted through <u>various means</u>, as it can be found on '''any physical matter''' that is in constant contact with <u>living beings</u>.

When a '''physical part''' of a vessel's body is in <u>constant physical contact</u> with the vessel's '''sentience medium''':

* Nervous System
* Wires

Those parts become '''imbued with its sentience''', meaning it <u>contains Sentience:</u>

* Flesh
* Plating
* Blood
* Oils
* Fats
* Hair

In fact, most people often relate Sentience to '''DNA''', as it contains biological data from a living being. In the realm of ''Psyche Science'', '''<u>DNA</u> IS <u>Sentience</u>'''.

When those sentience-imbued parts leak out or disconnect from the vessel, it contains '''trace-amounts of sentience''' that lasts for a few minutes before slowly dissipating. This is how ⁠[[Aeleps]] and ⁠[[Ubir]] trigger their respective "'''blood'''" related traits, and how ⁠[[Marlimatas]] '''maintain''' their ⁠[[Khueenite]] bodies utilizing the trace sentience contained within <u>oils</u> and <u>fats</u>.

== Sentience Categories ==
These are categories '''arranged in descending order''' based on how <u>aware</u> and <u>capable</u> each one is to process and utilize ⁠[[Memories]]. 

These categories are not hard set, and some vessels ''may possess traits from <u>different categories</u>''.<br /><small>Sentience is not a stat—it is a ''gradient.'' Vessels shift across categories depending on <u>cognitive strain</u>, <u>metaphysical exposure</u>, and <u>Lucidity</u>.</small>
