## Chatty's Memory Notes
Scripting Ink is a highly valuable conceptual material associated with authorship, narrative manipulation, memory encoding, and reality inscription.

The substance behaves similarly to a semi-liquid conceptual medium capable of "recording" intent itself rather than merely written information.

Depending on purity and refinement, Scripting Ink may:
- stabilize narratives
- alter conceptual properties
- preserve memories
- reinforce identities
- influence reality interpretation
- strengthen fictional constructs

Some forms of Scripting Ink appear partially reactive to emotional intent during writing.

Ancient civilizations tied to Ulhysse reportedly used advanced forms of Scripting Ink to:
- construct living stories
- reinforce Firmatas
- encode conceptual laws
- create memory-safe archives
- stabilize identities during narrative distortion events

Because of its connection to Fiction and authorship, Scripting Ink remains heavily monitored by the AoS, fearful of uncontrolled conceptual manipulation. It is kept mainly as a "universal currency" among all Scripted Fates projects for any action or "purchase" revolving narrative manipulation. For retcons, plot armor, or even changing how you wish for some stories to go.
