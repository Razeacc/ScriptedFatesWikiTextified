## Chatty's Memory Notes
Marks are a conceptualized currency system associated heavily with Pennypall Pincher and transactional game structures.
Unlike ordinary currencies, Marks partially derive value from perceived agreement, symbolic ownership, wager intent, and psychological commitment.

This causes certain Mark systems to behave unpredictably under conceptual influence.

In some games and exchanges:
- belief affects value
- emotional investment influences outcomes
- narrative significance alters worth
- symbolic ownership becomes binding

Pennypall Pincher heavily weaponizes these properties through games involving:
- wagers
- probabilistic manipulation
- psychological pressure
- exploitative loopholes
- conceptual contracts

At higher levels of play, Marks begin functioning less like money and more like weaponized agreement systems.
