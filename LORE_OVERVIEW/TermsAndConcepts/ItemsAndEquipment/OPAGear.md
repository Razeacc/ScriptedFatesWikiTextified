# OPA Exo-Gear
## 1/7 Operation Arsenal Platform
After countless time spent developing, modifying, and personalizing 
this gear— the AoS' <color=#92FFAB>OPA Exo Gear</color> is an agent's <b>primary method of
traversal</b> during operations. 
It acts as tool bed for <u>all the equipment an AoS Agent needs</u> when out on
missions, while maintaining secrecy and communication.
OPA Gears are specialized for its respective operation area to balance total anonimity and operational effectiveness tailor-fit to the space.

For instance, on the Udyverse timeline, the AoS branch there used a local material called "Thalassium" to replace Spirite. As for in "HER Firmata" during the events of Firmata Frontier and Frontline Firmata, Steel Sanctuary (The AoS' secret proxy puppet group) used steam instead of spirite instead, due to a lack of advanced processing and refining capabilities.

## 2/7 Unique Mask
It provides basic Fracturing and NulGraven protection. Its design mimics that of plague doctor masks, with added features depending on the wearer.
For ubir, gas mask filters and veil covering the eyes have been installed. The filters allows the manual-toggle of a blood scent to trigger blood rage at any time, while the veil allows the ubir to focus entirely on the scent of enemies, rather than vision, to navigate, makign them immune to effects that can influence eyesight.
Meanwhile, aeleps get visors on the mask's eye section to allow proper sight and enhanced range capabilities. It has smaller filters, to provide ventilation and a constant flow of "blood scent" to keep them calm.

## 3/7 Core Systems
To avoid people stealing and using the gear, is electrical system utilize <color=#92FFAB>3142Hz-encrypted energy</color>.
A battery pack on its rear core socket contains this energy.

The socket can be opened up with the hatch handle, allowing for relatively-easy battery replacement.

Two <color=#67ABF1>Spirite</color> canisters behind the shoulders fine-tune the grappling mechanisms; its cables stretch up to the arm brace.<br>
A powered harness extends and wraps around the whole body— evenly distributing energy for equipment and kinetic forces from High-G maneuvers.

## 4/7 Arm Bracers
Essentially an agent's "phone", it has a PDA built in to allow for communication and monitoring of exo-gear systems.
The PDA attached to the bracer allows for quick communications to the operational wing, the Avalon City HeadQuarters, and nearby agents, all while using <color=#92FFAB>3142Hz</color>-encrypted channels.

A rod can extends from below the wrist, where the cable extends into. This connects it to the compatible equipment, for energy transfer and grappling maneuvers.
The hand is also reinforced with a miniature gauntlet-like structure behind it, which acts like an exosuit to provide additional muscle and kinetic support—
mainly for CQE combat and throwing grappling picks

## 5/7 Grappling Picks
Sliding an arm bracers rod into its socket, it connects the pick to the brace and the rest of the OPA Gear— allowing an agent to stay armed even when letting go of the tool.
The pick utilizes the gear's grappling cable, connecting it to the OPA's wiring and allowing it to function like a grappling hook.

It's segmented into three sections: The handle, the body, and the blade.
The <b>Handle</b>'s trigger <u>separates</u> it from the body, which is connected via ball bearing, allowing for full 360° rotations and making <u>swinging</u> it around easy.
The <b>Body</b> mainly connects the OPA's cable to the pick's cable while providing overall <u>stability</u>.
And The <b>Blade</b> is what <u>digs or tangles</u> onto surfaces.

## 6/7 Kinetic Greaves
The largest problem with high movement, is stopping suddenly without injury.
Although the muscle enhancement and grappling systems cost energy, the largest energy drain in the OPA, are its emergency landing boots.

They propel kinetic energy away from solid ground when experiencing high speeds. This slows down the agent just enough to avoid breaking their legs.
And if that's not enough, emergency systems in the form of bellow-like limb parts across the calve extend downwards to further ease an agent's kinetic energy as a last-ditch effort to slow them down safely

## 7/7 Barrel Guns
By slotting the bracer's rod into the gun chassis, it gives the Barrel Guns similar free-hand motion like the Grappling Pick. 
It uses a similar slot mechanic to equip different barrels.

Firing uses <color=#92FFAB>energy</color> to ignite a spark in the barrel, while <color=#67ABF1>spirite</color> plasma is expelled for further propulsion.
<u>Barrel Guns</u> are a <b><u>hybrid</u> <color=#92FFAB>3142Hz Energy</color> and <color=#67ABF1>spirite</color> firearm</b>.
When out of ammo, releasing the trigger detaches the barrel for another to be loaded in.
Barrels hang above the knee, allowing for quick sliding motions to reload rapidly.

The harness and rod reduce recoil from firing by transferring its kinetics throughout the whole body.

## Extra/7 RTE (Rapid Teleportation Equipment)
Optional for agents operating outside of timelines, or in highly-advanced spaces, the RTE can be easily equipped and fastened onto an Agent's OPA gear to allow for Rapid Teleportation.
This slots into the harness, allowing it to fully read the user's proportions. Then, through the use of intent, the Agent can then fly upwards, in all 3 dimensions, at free will. Zero air resistance, zero gravity, zero inertia... ok, perhaps there's SOME air resistance when RT'ing through windy locations or nearby singularities.
But for the most part, due to this equipment relying on rapid teleportation in a certain direction rather than propulsion or any other form of movement, it creates an almost uncanny but highly-efficient form of movement, where agents can be seen flying around at high speeds while maintaining a completely static and barely aero-dynamic position, allowing them to prioritize strategic poses and actions over "what pose would make me move faster?".
