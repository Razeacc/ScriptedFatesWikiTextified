Often known as "The 4 Fundamental Aspects of Reality", they are the '''building blocks''' of fiction.

<br />{{AspectIcon|1}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Space|Space]] and {{AspectIcon|2}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Time|TIME]] work closely with {{AspectIcon|3}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Control|CONTROL]] to form the realm of <u>Existence</u>.<br />Meanwhile, {{AspectIcon|4}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|Phantasy]] enlists aid from the ''vibrations'' of {{AspectIcon|3}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Control|CONTROL]]. It two-times reality with this 4th aspect to help form <u>Non-Existence</u>.<br />Together, all 4 aspects form the lands we walk on, and can't walk on.

<small>In the past, and partially in the present, they were '''manipulated''' by [[Archkin]] to perform <u>miracles</u> and <u>enact</u> their will.</small>

== Aspectual Applications ==
Think of these as the "'''type'''" of abilities  that Aspect Manipulation can do. While the aspects domain over certain parts of reality, applications are what the aspects do to these parts of reality.

These types aren't '''hard-set''', some abilities can be considered under <u>multiple applications</u> at once as there are many different formulas that can bring you to the same solution.<br />If you feel and ability fits better under a different application, your thought is valid and aren't wrong to think so. You're likely to reach the same effect regardless.

<blockquote style="border-left:3px solid #00FF72;padding-left:8px;">
{{Material_Template|title1=Time|image1=TimeAspect.png|type=Moments and Destiny|color=Green}}

=== Temporal Bubbles ===
'''WARNING''':<br />When time in a region <u>de-syncs</u> from the time <u>outside</u> the region, a '''temporal bubble forms'''.<br />Once the bubble bursts <small>(when the time manipulation ends)</small>, the <u>closer something is to the edge</u>, the more '''damage severity''' it would experience as time quickly catches up/slows down to match the larger body.<br />

<small>Due to how bizarre the concept of time is, this is the '''least common''' aspect utilized by [[Archkin]]. In fact, it's normal for attempts to manipulate [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Time|TIME]] to <u>randomly fail</u>, even with the most skilled of users. This is the only aspect that actively resists manipulation, as its structure is self-correcting. But, there is another reason...</small></blockquote>

== [[Phantasy]] ==
{{Material_Template|title1=Phantasy|image1=PhantasyAspect.png|type=Abstract and Nothingness|color=Cyan / Red}}
From the manifestations of your '''mind''' and the '''thoughts''' that race in your head, to the thinking process of one's '''creativity''' and the overwhelming dread of '''regret'''. 

{{AspectIcon|4}} '''[[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|Phantasy]]''' is easily the '''most complicated aspect''' as it almost single-handedly governs the entirety of '''non-existence''' and '''abstract''' nature, only gaining assistance from {{AspectIcon|3}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Control|CONTROL]] in order to maintain the '''frequencies''' of [[PSYCHE (Soul)]] phenomena and [[Memories#Memory Amplitude|'''amplitude''']] of [[Memories]]. Outside of the vibrations from Souls, you can tell when <u>pure phantasmal phenomena</u> is occurring when you're in the presence of any '''2413Hz''' frequencies. 

On a first glance, most assume <u>Non-Existence</u> is the ''absence of Existence''. While partially true, in full contextuality, it's the realm of '''unmaterialized ideas''', '''concepts''', '''[[Memories|memories]]''', '''dreams''', '''emotions''', and [[PSYCHE (Soul)]].

To properly handle such a monumental pillar, this aspect is split into <u>two halves</u>. 
----

=== [[Fracturing]] ===
<blockquote style="border-left:3px solid #00FFFF;padding-left:8px;">
This half acts as the '''thinking''' mind. '''Fracturing''' handles <u>[[Memories]]</u>, <u>logic</u>, <u>mathematics</u>, <u>quantum</u> phenomena, <u>probability</u>, and <u>rational reasoning</u>. It is noted for acting similar to an '''echo''' but for [[memories]] that have '''ceased to exist'''.
</blockquote>

=== [[NulGraven]] ===
<blockquote style="border-left:3px solid #FF0000;padding-left:8px;">
This half acts as the '''feeling''' mind. '''NulGraven''' handles <u>[[Sentience]]</u>, <u>imagination</u>, <u>emotion</u>, <u>dreams</u>, <u>instincts</u>, and <u>spiritual phenomena</u>. It is noted for acting similar to a '''virus''' or '''feral animal''' as it often mirrors the <u>most primal emotions</u> of all: '''Fear''', '''Anger''', and '''Hunger'''.
</blockquote>

=== Phantasmal Unity ===
Despite the split between both halves, they <u>often share dominions</u> among each other, as if this very aspect is a '''thinking''' and '''conscious being'''; a <u>'''living aspect'''</u>. Although one half may control a trait, it may <u>occasionally be controlled by the other half</u> as well. The most notable dominion '''both''' control is [[PSYCHE (Soul)]].<blockquote>
<small>Due to the nature of Phantasy, [[Phantasmal Entities]], and how this aspect is dangerously hostile towards them— this is the '''most taboo''' aspect for [[archkin]] to practice.</small></blockquote>
