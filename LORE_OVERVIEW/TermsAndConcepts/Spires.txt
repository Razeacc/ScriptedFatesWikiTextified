Chatty's Additional Notes:
Spires function similarly to geological pressure release points for Spirite deep beneath The Infinite Fields.

Although visually similar to volcanic eruptions or massive energy pillars, Spires are natural phenomena in this world and appear subtly tilted eastward regardless of surrounding terrain.

Many researchers theorize this universal tilt occurs because all Spires are subconsciously "reaching" toward The 4th's Wall.

Spirite Leylines function as underground channels carrying volatile Spirite across massive distances beneath continents and Exspanses.

These flowing currents gradually transmutate nearby stone and minerals over long periods, explaining why many rare materials naturally form near ancient leyline paths.

Entire ecosystems, civilizations, and migration patterns historically formed around stable Spirite flow regions.
