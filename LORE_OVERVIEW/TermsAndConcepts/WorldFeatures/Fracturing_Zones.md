## Idea
> Spaces where memories slowly grow dull as they're absorbed by nearby Fracturing Fissures. A dark cyan tint fills the room as the air vibrates a sickening 2413Hz frequency.
> Fracturing Zones are ghost-like spaces where the Fracturing bleeds into reality. They have several tiers that affect how strong the zone is, and what kinds of occurances can happen within.

## Tier 0
> Fissures: 1
When a single fissure is made, it's mostly meant merely to contain an archkin, or other similar frequency-attuned creature, on the spot.
It carries no other major threat, unless a vicar arrives to summon reinforcements from it in the form of Fracturing Caradels.

## Tier 1
> Fissures: 2-3
This is when the ambiance starts shifting the cyan as fissure flies float around. It becomes easier to call in more caradels as the zone itself passively fabricates point caradels and fracturing mirages.

## Tier 2
> 4-8 Fissures
Locus and Polygonal Caradels start passively fabricating around the fissures as the air starts vibrating.

## Tier 3
> 9-15 Fissures
Rogue Caradels can now spawn passively. Electrical interferance can now happen. Jamming, disabling, or general instability of electrical appliances can now happen in these zones.

## Tier 4
> 16-20 Fissures
FissureQuakes can now happen as Thronus caradels can now control the terrain. Fissure Quakes happen when a Thronus arrives and leaves. The quake takes rooms and other small spaces in the zone, and swaps them around one-another.
The result: Confusion, more intense memory drainage, and denser vibrations of 2413Hz frequencies.


# Unique Types
## Stabilized Zones
When a large fissure is made and connected with a Memory Piece, the Fracturing Zone can stabilize and create a recreation of the scene portrayed in the memory piece. Fracturing Mirages take further priority in passive fabrication, especially if they're mirages from the memory piece. It is also safe to traverse here, as the Fracturing Zone is essentially "satiated" by the Memory Piece. It doesn't even consume it, rather, treating it as its "core".
However, this phenomena can only happen when using "strong enough" Memory Pieces, such as those from Archkin or similar long-lived species with good memory. An average memory piece would simply be consumed by the zone.

## Decanoluzion Zones
When a story is abandoned by its author, its world becomes subjected to a massive world-wide "Red" Fracturing Zone known as a "Decanoluzion Zone". The red tint is due to the massive number of sentience being repurposed and destroyed as the space gets dragged into a Decanoluzioned Fiction.
This is a highly sensitive and cataclysmic apocalyptic scenario.

## Fracturing Veils
Exclusive to Project: FOURTH MARSHAL, these are specialized "Stabilized Zones" that tap into Decanoluzioned Fiction. Essentially, a version of the "Alternate Fiction", but instead of simulating the "Decanoluzioned fictions", it brings them into the physical plane through the use of hostile tangible Fracturing Mirages. They're also much smaller, being incapable of bleeding actual terrain and environments into the physical world, unlike Stabilized and Decanoluzion Zones.
What makes them especially dangerous, is how these mirages can simply "bleed out" of reality to become inangible to physical attacks, meaning you'd need something phantasmal to reliably damage them.
