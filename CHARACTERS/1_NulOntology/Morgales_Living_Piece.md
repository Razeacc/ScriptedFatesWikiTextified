## IDEA
> Initially created as part of An-Tri'Fhiana's soul experiments, "Her Living Piece" was assigned as the new defacto leader of Outpost 3 after Morgale decided to continue on with the exodus.
> She has most of Morgale's memories, but mainly her love for "fiction" and other worlds: Timelines.
> Morga's Living Piece was obsessed with studying the timelines above her, wishing she could bring those characters over to her world, but knew such consequences would be dire.
> 
> Little did she know, after becoming a Firmata, would those earlier dreams of her come true in the form of Otherworlders during the events of Firmata Frontier.
